Insurance & System Navigation

Medicaid, marketplace plans, postpartum coverage gaps, and the appeals processes that can save your client's coverage. The system is designed to confuse — your job is to help her navigate it.

Scope of PracticeYou do not provide insurance advice. You understand the landscape and connect clients with navigators, enrollment specialists, and legal aid.

The Coverage Gap

Until recently, Medicaid coverage ended 60 days postpartum — the most dangerous period for maternal morbidity and mortality. The American Rescue Plan extended postpartum Medicaid to 12 months in states that opted in. Know your state's status. Emergency Medicaid covers labor and delivery regardless of immigration status. CHIP covers children in families above Medicaid thresholds.

What You Can Do

  • Know if your state has extended postpartum Medicaid to 12 months
  • Help clients find marketplace navigators at healthcare.gov
  • Connect undocumented clients with emergency Medicaid and FQHC clinics
  • Know that pregnancy is a qualifying life event for marketplace enrollment
  • Help with Medicaid renewal paperwork — many lose coverage due to administrative churn
