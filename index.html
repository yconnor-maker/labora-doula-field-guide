Physician Notes
Scope of Practice Notice This educational material is designed to enhance clinical literacy for birthworkers operating within their defined scope of practice. Nothing in this guide expands a doula's legal scope of practice, authorizes diagnosis, or substitutes for medical training. When in doubt, escalate — never diagnose, treat, or prescribe.
